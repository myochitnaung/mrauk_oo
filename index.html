An Ancient City ( Mrauk U )

In 1433, King Min Saw Mon established Mrauk U as the capital of the last unified Arakanese Kingdom.The city eventually reached a size of 160,000 in the early seventeenth century.[8] Mrauk U served as the capital of the Mrauk U kingdom and its 49 kings till the conquest of the kingdom by the Burmese Konbaung Dynasty in 1784.read more
